## I. Subject Matter and Purpose

1. Due to the need for expansion of the 北航 hub data center and the upgrade of the departure system, Party A purchases the following equipment from Party B for use in the computer rooms at the Guangzhou headquarters and the Shanghai Hongqiao base of 北方航空.  
2. The specifications of this batch of equipment are aligned with those of the concurrent tender of “Dongnan Airlines” (China Dongnan Airlines Co., Ltd.) solely for the purpose of technical benchmarking. This does not constitute any rights or obligations of “Dongnan Airlines” towards Party A.

## II. Equipment List (Summary)

| 1   | PC Server       | 2U rack-mounted, dual CPU, ≥512GB memory                | 40 units |
| 2   | Core Switch     | 100G uplink, redundant power supply                     | 8 units  |
| 3   | Access Switch   | 25G downlink, POE+                                      | 24 units |
| 4   | Firewall        | Next-generation firewall, dual-machine hot standby      | 2 sets   |

## III. Delivery and Acceptance

1. Party B shall deliver the goods to the warehouse designated by 北航 in Guangzhou no later than 2026 年 6 月 30 日.  
2. Party A is entitled to engage a third‑party testing institution to participate in the acceptance. If the specifications do not conform to the contract, 北方航空 may refuse to accept the goods and require Party B to replace them within 15 days.  

## IV. Price and Payment

The total contract price including tax is RMB 壹仟贰佰捌拾万元整 (¥12,800,000.00). Party A shall pay in two installments: 40% after contract signing, and 60% after the goods have passed acceptance.  

## V. Confidentiality and Publicity

Without the prior written consent of 华云智联, 北航 shall not disclose Party B’s quotation details in press releases; nor may Party B, in external publicity, use expressions such as “exclusive supplier of 北航”.  

## VI. Miscellaneous

This Contract is made in four originals, of which 北方航空 holds two and 华云智联 holds two, all having the same legal effect.
